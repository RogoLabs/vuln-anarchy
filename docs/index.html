The Vulnerability Data Integrity Gap

Visualizing scoring divergence between NVD and GitHub Advisory databases to identify prioritization risks.

Vulnerability management depends on accurate severity scoring — but authoritative sources frequently provide conflicting CVSS data. The Drift Score is the absolute variance (|GitHub − NVD|) representing the delta in perceived risk between the two providers. A high Drift Score indicates a significant discrepancy in risk assessment, suggesting that relying on either source alone may lead to misaligned patching priorities.
